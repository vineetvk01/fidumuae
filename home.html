About Us

FIDUM is a diversified consultancy specializing in Development Management, Project Management, Cost, Quality and Time management for the Construction Industry. We apply leading practices as a market discriminator, and seek clients and strategic partners with a shared commitment to performance, quality, and success.

Our team has over 30 years of experience and have worked on projects with scopes such as master planned communities, international airports, manufacturing, commercial and residential construction, and numerous theme developments. With global expertise in Strategy and Leadership, Project Management, and Engineering - we bring the full strength of our integrated capabilities to the table for every client; often assisting clients in one sector or industry adopt success solutions from another.

Services

Development Management

We undertake everything from feasibility study, business case preparation, business case management, etc. to project budget management and project program supervision.

Project Management

Our PM services include Master Planning, full life-cycle of Pre-Contract and Post-Contract planning, engineering, and inspection for major design and construction projects.

Contracts Management

Preparation of contract documents, cost control, procurement, and coordination between client, contractor and sub-contractors are some of the tasks we will shoulder.

Project Control Group Reports

PCG Reports are the tools for involvement of all project stake holders from Top Management to Middle Management personnel to make sure each personnel delivers the agreed requirements.

Construction Management

We provide high quality client representation for completion of projects on-time and within budget. We also work with leading-edge technologies for application of advanced information and communication technology to the project development industry.
